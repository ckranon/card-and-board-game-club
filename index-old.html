Dungeons & Dragons

We love D&D, but we also respect your time and our DMs' energy. That is why we run D&D in tiers:

One-Shot Sessions

Show up, pick a pre-made character, and play a complete 2–3 hour adventure. No homework, no session zero, no commitment. Perfect for newcomers and busy schedules.

Campaign Arcs

A short story told over 3–5 sessions. Enough depth to care about your character, short enough to finish before midterms.

Full Campaigns

Level 1–20 epics happen only when a dedicated group and DM commit for the long haul. These are co-op ventures, not solo DM marathons.

Want to DM? Talk to Christian (VP). We provide pre-made modules, dice, and moral support.
